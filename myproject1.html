    • Bachelor's Thesis:

    EVALUATION OF HARMONICS AND FILTER DESIGN FOR INDUSTRIAL LOADS

    Since few past years we saw the fast increase of power electronics based loads connected to the power system. These kinds of loads draw non-sinusoidal current which causes injection of harmonics in power system. Harmonics have several disadvantages in which the main is that it degrades the power quality. This thesis evaluates the harmonics and methods to mitigate it and proposed a shunt active filter in solution of harmonics and to mitigate it in allowable conditions set by standards.
